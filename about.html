Our Mission

At China Town Resto, our mission is to bring the authentic flavors of Chinese cuisine to our community while providing exceptional dining experiences. We are driven by our passion for food and hospitality, aiming to exceed the expectations of our guests with every visit.

Our team is dedicated to upholding our core values of quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We strive to create a welcoming environment where guests can indulge in delicious dishes crafted from the freshest ingredients and served with care and attention to detail.

Beyond serving delicious meals, we are committed to making a positive impact in the community. Through partnerships with local organizations and initiatives, we contribute to the well-being and development of our neighborhood, fostering a sense of belonging and unity.

Our History

China Town Resto was established in 2005 by the Wong family, immigrants from Guangzhou, China, who brought with them a rich culinary heritage and a passion for sharing their culture through food. Inspired by the bustling street markets and vibrant flavors of their hometown, they set out to create a dining destination that would capture the essence of authentic Chinese cuisine.

From humble beginnings as a small family-owned restaurant, China Town Resto has grown into a beloved establishment cherished by locals and visitors alike. Over the years, we have overcome various challenges and obstacles, always staying true to our commitment to excellence and authenticity.

Our journey has been marked by memorable moments and milestones, from receiving prestigious culinary awards to expanding our menu offerings and enhancing our dining experience. Each chapter of our history is a testament to the dedication and perseverance of our team and the unwavering support of our loyal patrons.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to honoring our heritage while embracing innovation and growth. We are grateful for the opportunity to serve our community and look forward to many more years of sharing delicious meals, creating cherished memories, and celebrating the culinary delights of China Town Resto.
